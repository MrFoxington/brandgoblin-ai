# Airo Landing Brief V4 — Freemium reposition (kill the 7-day trial)

### THE SHIFT: no more "7 days of full Creator Pro free." New model = a lasting FREE tier (create a brand +
### try Goblin Studio free + free Nix goodies) and **Creator Pro ($19/mo) when you're ready to GROW.**
### Free users stick around and share → feeds distribution. A trial that hard-stops works against that.
> For the Airo AI editor (brandgoblinai.com). Site-wide messaging change + new pricing section.
> ⚠️ CONSISTENCY: the app must match (lasting free tier + free Studio energy taste, no 7-day hard trial).
> Ship this alongside the app's "Free Studio Taste + Upsell" change so the site never promises what the
> app doesn't do.

---

## 1. SITE-WIDE: remove ALL "7-day trial" language
Replace every reference to the 7-day trial / "7 days of everything" / "7 days of full Creator Pro" /
"What happens after the free trial" across the whole site (hero badge, CTA subtexts, pricing, FAQ).
New framing everywhere: **"Start free. Upgrade to Creator Pro when you're ready to grow."**
- CTA subtexts like "7 days of everything · no card" → **"Free to start · no credit card"**
- Hero badge "7 days of everything — free, no card" → **"Create your brand free — no credit card"**
- FAQ "What happens after the free trial?" → rewrite (see §4).

## 2. NEW PRICING SECTION
**Section headline:** Start free. Grow when you're ready.
**Subhead:** Create your brand and try Goblin Studio free — no credit card. Upgrade to Creator Pro when
you're ready to grow.

### Column A — FREE  ·  $0
*Everything you need to start.*
- Generate your brand — names, story, voice, colors & logo direction
- **Try Goblin Studio free** — real logos, social graphics & product art (Creative Energy included)
- Free Nix stickers & wallpapers
- No credit card, ever
- **CTA button:** Start Creating — Free

### Column B — CREATOR PRO  ·  $19/mo  ·  badge "Most Popular"
*Your AI marketing department — for when you're ready to grow.*
- Unlimited brand generations
- The full content engine — social posts, blogs, emails & ad copy
- **Monthly Creative Energy** for Goblin Studio (logos, graphics, product art)
- Ongoing marketing ideas & campaigns
- Top up Creative Energy anytime
- **CTA button:** Upgrade to Creator Pro

*Small note under the cards:* Need more Studio power? Top up Creative Energy anytime — no subscription required.

## 3. BE HONEST ABOUT STUDIO ENERGY (don't overpromise "unlimited")
"Unlimited" applies to brand kits + text content. **Goblin Studio image generation runs on Creative
Energy** (a monthly allowance on Pro, a starter amount free). Do NOT say "unlimited images." Frame Studio
as "Creative Energy included / monthly Creative Energy," so no one expects infinite image generation.

## 4. FAQ rewrite
Replace the "What happens after the free trial?" Q with:
**Q: Is it really free? What's the catch?**
A: Creating your brand and trying Goblin Studio is free — no credit card, no trial clock. You also get free
Nix stickers and wallpapers. When you're ready to grow — unlimited brands, the full content engine, and
more Creative Energy for the Studio — Creator Pro is $19/month. Cancel anytime.

## 5. KEEP
The strong, low-friction hook and the orange/primary CTAs. Keep "✦ Start Creating — Free" as the primary
CTA everywhere. Keep the showcase wall + Goblin Studio sections. Footer trust line stays
("No card required · Cancel anytime · Powered by Nix").
