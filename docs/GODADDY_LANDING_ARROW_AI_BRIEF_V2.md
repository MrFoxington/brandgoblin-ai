## STRATEGIC DIRECTION FOR V2 (the new north star)

**BrandGoblin does ONE thing brilliantly: turn an idea into a brand, fast, and make it so fun you
can't stop.** We are NOT a suite. Every unbuilt product we advertise is a tax on that message and a
vaporware promise we may never want to keep. So beyond the earlier fixes, V2 makes two **deletions**:

1. **DELETE the "See what BrandGoblin conjures" live-examples section.** It's a static canned demo
   that duplicates the Mana Brew before/after right above it and delivers zero dopamine (nobody
   *does* anything). The real magic is in the app. Keep one proof point (Mana Brew), cut the rest.
2. **DELETE the "Where Future Magic Is Forged" Goblin Labs six-product grid.** Studio, Motion,
   Sites, Growth, Marketplace are unbuilt — listing them is vaporware and dilutes focus. Remove the
   grid entirely.
3. **DELETE Agency Edition everywhere** — the pricing card, the waitlist line, and any nav/footer
   mentions. For now the page sells exactly **two** things: **Free Trial (7 days of everything)**
   and **Creator Pro ($19/mo)**. Nothing else.

We add other tools and tiers later, once paying customers ask for them. Right now: one product, one
upgrade, and the loop — **create → get hooked → come back daily to build more and refill energy.**
