---

## STEP 1 — Put up your website (the big one)
Your kit already wrote your website words for you. You just need somewhere to put them.

**Easiest path (recommended): let a website builder do it for you.**
1. Pick a website builder — these are made for total beginners (no coding):
   - **GoDaddy Airo**, **Wix**, **Squarespace**, or **Hostinger** — all let you get a web address AND
     build the site in one place.
2. Start a new site and, when it asks what your site is about, **paste in your BrandGoblin website copy**
   (hit "Copy" on the Website Copy section). Their AI builds the pages around your words.
3. Drop in your colors (from your palette) and your logo. Hit publish. You have a website. 🎉

**Even easier (if you used BrandGoblin's "Preview as Webpage"):**
1. In your kit, click **👁 Preview as Webpage**, then **⤓ Download HTML** — that's your whole site in one
   file.
2. Go to **app.netlify.com/drop** (free), drag that file onto the page, and it's live in seconds. You can
   connect a custom web address later.

**Getting your web address (your "domain"):** a domain is just your site's name, like *yourbrand.com*.
You can buy one (~$10–15/year) at GoDaddy, Namecheap, or right inside the builder you chose. Tip: keep it
short and match your brand name.

---

## STEP 2 — Get your logo
Your kit gave you a **logo prompt** — that's the exact description an AI needs to draw your logo.
- **Easiest:** generate it right inside BrandGoblin (Goblin Studio) — it uses your prompt + colors.
- **Or:** copy your logo prompt into any AI image tool and generate it there.
Save it as a PNG and use it on your site and socials.

---

## STEP 3 — Set up your socials
Your kit wrote your bios and your first posts.
1. Make accounts on the platforms you want (Instagram, TikTok, X, etc.).
2. Paste in the **bio** we wrote for each one.
3. Post your **launch posts** to announce you exist. (Copy is already done — just paste.)

---

## STEP 4 — Follow your 7-day launch plan
Your kit includes a **day-by-day launch plan**. Open it and just do today's step. That's it. One small
action a day for a week and you're officially launched.
