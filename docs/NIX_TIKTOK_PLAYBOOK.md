# Nix TikTok Playbook — make Nix feel real, turn attention into signups

### The mascot is the moat. This is the distribution engine. Goal: make people fall in love with Nix
### (a living little magical goblin who hangs out with Fox), then funnel that attention into the app.
> Strategy doc. Created June 22, 2026. Built from Fox's vision + 2025/26 TikTok virality research.

---

## NORTH STAR FOR THE CHANNEL
People don't follow software. They follow **characters and people they love.** We sell BrandGoblin by
making Nix feel **alive** — a real little creature (think Gizmo / Baby Yoda / a Smurf) who lives in
Fox's world, reacts, gets into mischief, and conjures brands. Fox is the charismatic human half;
Nix is the magic. The app is what Nix *does* — shown, never lectured.

---

## ⛔ THE ONE RULE THAT PROTECTS ALL OF THIS — KEEP NIX ON-MODEL
A mascot only works if he's **instantly recognizable every single time.** Recognition = consistency.
That means:
- **Always animate FROM Fox's approved Nix art** (image-to-video / motion-transfer using the real Nix
  PNG as the source). NEVER let AI invent a fresh Nix from a text prompt — he'll drift off-model
  (wrong green, wrong hair, lost hoodie) and break the spell.
- Quality gate before posting any Nix frame: green skin, purple hair, pointed ears, purple "NIX"
  hoodie with gold trim. If he's off-model in a clip, the clip doesn't ship.
- This is the same hard rule as the app, applied to marketing: **Nix's design always originates from
  Fox's canonical art.** Animation is fine; reinvention is not.

> This is non-negotiable because it's literally the asset. An off-model Nix is a stranger.

## WHAT ACTUALLY GOES VIRAL (2025/26 research — design every video around this)
1. **The first 3 seconds are everything.** Videos holding 60%+ of viewers past 3s get ~4x the reach;
   70–85% retention = ~2.2x views. Open ON the payoff or the hook — no intros, no logos, no "hey guys."
2. **Completion rate > likes.** Short videos (<15s) hit ~92% completion and punch way above their weight.
   Keep most videos 7–15s. Earn longer ones only when the story justifies it.
3. **Identity-call hooks score highest.** Open by naming the viewer + a promise:
   "POV: you have a business idea but zero design skills…" / "If you've ever wanted a logo in 60 seconds…"
4. **Sound + on-screen text + hashtags = categorization.** ~80% of viral videos are correctly
   categorized by the algorithm via trending sound, captions, and on-screen keywords. Always use a
   trending or original sound, burn keywords into the first text frame, and tag the niche.
5. **Personality beats polish.** Duolingo/CeraVe won with humor, self-deprecation, and culture — not
   production value. Phone-shot + charisma + a lovable character > a glossy ad every time.

## THE FORMULA (every video)
**HOOK (0–3s)** stop-scroll visual or identity call →
**PAYOFF/STORY (3–12s)** Nix + Fox do the funny/magical thing →
**SOFT CTA (last 1–2s or caption)** "Nix made this in the app — link in bio" / "Comment a business idea
and Nix will brand it."

## CONTENT PILLARS (rotate these — this is the variety engine)

**1. Nix Lives Here (the heart — make him feel real)**
Fox in the real world, Nix animated into the scene, alive and mischievous.
- Coffee shop: Nix sits on the cup rim stirring espresso with a tiny fork.
- Fox working on laptop; Nix paces across the keyboard, "reading" the screen, reacting.
- Travel: Nix peeks out of a backpack pocket, waves at landmarks, hides when a waiter comes.
- Nix "asleep" in a hoodie pocket, yawns, stretches. Pure cute. These build *love*, not clicks — and
  love is what makes people follow and share.

**2. Mirror Mode (your dance/action idea — high viral ceiling)**
Fox does a dance move or action; Nix is overlaid and mirrors it (motion-transfer/puppeteering tech).
- Fox + Nix do a trending dance in sync.
- Fox points, Nix copies; Fox trips, Nix face-plants. Duet-bait and remix-bait — huge for reach.

**3. Conjure It (the product, disguised as magic)**
Nix actually makes a brand on screen — the "wow in 60 seconds."
- "Comment a business idea →" then Nix conjures the logo + kit live. (Reply-to-comment videos are a
  growth loop — each comment is a new video.)
- Before/after: napkin scribble → full brand kit. Satisfying, screenshot-able.

**4. Fox the Founder (you, the charismatic human)**
Build-in-public + personality. Platform-dependent: lean heavier here on YouTube/IG, lighter on TikTok.
- "I'm building an app where a goblin makes your whole brand." Honest, funny, founder-journey.
- React to wild creations users (or you) make. You + Nix riffing together.

**5. Trend Hijack (speed plays)**
Whatever sound/format is hot this week, run it through Nix + Fox within 24–48h. Trends decay fast;
being early is most of the win.

## CADENCE (first 30 days — adjust to what's sustainable)
- **Target: 1 post/day**, the proven solo-founder sweet spot. If that's too much, 4–5/week, never <3.
- **Volume is how you learn what hits.** Most videos won't pop; you're farming for the one that does,
  then you make ten more of that one.
- Batch-produce: shoot/generate a week of Nix clips in one session so daily posting is just editing +
  captioning, not starting from zero.
- Post in the evening window for your audience; reply to every comment in the first hour (early
  engagement is a ranking signal).

## CTA STRATEGY (turn attention → signups, without killing the vibe)
- **Entertain first, sell light.** ~80% pure-love/entertainment, ~20% direct "try it." If every video
  is an ad, growth stalls.
- Primary CTA lives in **bio link + comment**, not shouted in-video. "Nix is in the app — link in bio."
- The strongest funnel: **reply-to-comment videos** ("you asked, Nix made it") — they prove the product
  AND generate endless content AND drive comments (reach). This is the money loop.
- Pin a comment with the link on every video.

---

## METRICS THAT MATTER (ignore vanity)
- **3-second retention** (the make-or-break — watch this above all).
- **Average watch time / completion %** (aim 80%+).
- **Shares + saves** (love signals — predict follows).
- **Profile clicks → bio link clicks → signups** (the actual business result).
- Likes/views are downstream noise; don't optimize for them.
