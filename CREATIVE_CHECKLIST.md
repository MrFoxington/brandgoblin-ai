## 🎬 THE UNLOCK — Rig Nix (do this first; everything waits on it)
Full step-by-step: `docs/NIX_RIG_WALKTHROUGH.md`. The rig is the one-weekend hump; after it, clips are 15–45 min each.

- [ ] **Pick the source art** — a clean front-facing Nix PNG (arms slightly out, neutral mouth, transparent bg)
- [ ] **Part 1 — Cut Nix into layers** in Photopea (head/hair/ears/eyes/mouth shapes/torso/arms/legs), paint behind limbs, name layers exactly, export `nix-puppet.psd`
- [ ] **Part 2 — Free proof in Adobe Character Animator** (Starter mode): import PSD → auto-rig → webcam Body Tracker → watch Nix mirror you → record + export a test clip
- [ ] **Decision point:** did Nix mirror you and stay on-model? (if off, send a frame to the `nix-animator` agent)
- [ ] **Part 3 — Cartoon Animator 5** (free trial): import the same PSD → drag a library dance onto Nix → export
- [ ] **Part 4 — Composite into your world** in CapCut (Nix onto your shoulder / coffee cup, add shadow)
- [ ] 🎉 **First real on-model Nix clip exported** — the strategy is proven
